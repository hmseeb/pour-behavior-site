Our Story

Where Every Pour Tells a Story

Pour Behavior was born from a passion for the craft of mixology and an obsession with detail. We believe that a great cocktail is more than a drink — it's a moment, a memory, a conversation starter.

Located in the heart of Liepāja, our bar brings together the finest spirits, freshest ingredients, and an intimate atmosphere where close friends discover something special in every glass.

From classic builds reimagined to original creations inspired by the seasons, each cocktail is designed with intention and served with care.
